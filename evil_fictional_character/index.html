Seryu Ubiquitous
Seryu Ubiquitous was a member of the organization Jaegers and a former member of the Imperial Guard. She was a young girl who possessed a strong sense of justice to the point of to become a delusional psychotic obsessed with a distorted justice.

Age: 20

Gender: Female

Height: 5'4

Status: Decease

Faction: Jaegers, Imperial Guard (formerly)

Teigu: Hekatonkheires
